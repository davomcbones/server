Fish

Just some random stuff about my favorite fish

I discovered most of these fish from my interest in fish keeping, I will try my best to give credits to the photos I used below

Leopard Danio

While not the fanciest of fish leopard danios are still pretty good fish. They are native to the waters of India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh The make great beginner fish for fishkeeping and are easy to breed, they enjoy swimming together in shoals

Half Banded Spiny eel (Gnarp)

The Half Banded Spiny eel (macrognathus circumcinctus), sometimes nicknamed the gnarp is a very pretty eel. In the wild they are seen in lakes across Africa and Asia, they have a very fun personality and are known to be noodling around in the plants, watching the other fish, snuffling for food and sleeping under substrate or other decor. Photographed by arcstarx

Pygmy Corydora

Pygmy Corydoras are one of the world's smallest catfish, they also like swimming in big groups and are a great addition to smaller community aquariums

Dojo Loach

Another cool eel looking fish, very fun and very pretty.

Medaka Rice Fish

Rice Fish are among the most recommended beginner fish for fishkeeping, and they come in a huge variatey of colours and patterns

Platy

Just like ricefish, Platies come in many colours and patterns. They are also livebearers, meaning they give birth to live baby fish instead of laying eggs

Featherfin squeaker (Synodontus eupterus)

Synodontus eupterus, or Featherfin Squeaker is a corydora that is larger than a bronchis. They are usually not referred to as their common name and have the ability to make noises to communicate with one another. They are also known for their high feather-like fin which is valued by many fish keeping hobbyists. Photographed by arcstarx
