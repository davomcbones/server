About CycleSwap

CycleSwap a school project buisness founded by us in Catholic Cathedral College, Christchurch. We are team of two people, with volunteers from friends and family. Our buisness is small, but we have a big vision!

Our biggest goal is to leave a positive mark to the community. Achieving this by providing an affordable option of transportation, while being as sustainable as possible

We are part of the YES program!

CycleSwap is run by local students as part of our school buisness project and is partnered by the YES program (Young Enterprise Scheme)

Refurbished to our standards

Our bikes we sell here in CycleSwap are restored and refurbished by us so our customers recieve the bikes at their best condition possible. We offer Road bikes, Mountain bikes, even Scooters! Big and small we got them all
