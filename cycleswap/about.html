About CycleSwap

CycleSwap a school project buisness founded by us in Catholic Cathedral College, Christchurch. We are team of two people, with volunteers from friends and family. Our buisness is small, but we have a big vision!

Our biggest goal is to leave a positive mark to the community. Achieving this by providing an affordable option of transportation, while being as sustainable as possible
